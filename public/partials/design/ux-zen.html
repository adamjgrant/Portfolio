UX Zen was a project of passion. I wanted to keep my Twitter feed active and share the things I found that made me feel good about being a UX Designer.

I wanted this to be more than just a automatic link-sharer. I waited a long time before starting the project, collecting 365 quotations and links that really spoke to me.

The home screen shows a borderless immersion of great design and ideas. The sidebar populates with a random selection of "zens" from those that are published.

At the same time, I had been collecting my favorite of the favorite visual designs into a folder so I could browse over them now and then for inspiration.

Once I reached 365 "zens," I started the design by pulling from the photoset and automatically scheduling the posts to publish daily and tweet to my Twitter account.

Each quotation is set in this beautiful thin display font.


Once I hit 365, I'm not sure if I'll create another 365 or curate the previous list for the best of the best and fill in the gaps.

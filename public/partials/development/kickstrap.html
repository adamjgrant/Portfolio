Kickstrap started as a simple personal tool made for my own use and happened to host on GitHub. To my surprise, I found many people had found it, without any promotion on my part. Later it found itself on the front page of Hacker News twice and quickly grew to over 1,500 stars on GitHub.

It remains an active project now under development by myself and two other geniuses. The second version is currently under active development, due in time for the Bootstrap 3 release. This very site uses a work-in-progress of Kickstrap 2 with the current unreleased Bootstrap 3.

One of the first businesses to use Kickstrap was SkuVault, a feature-robust tool for warehouse management.
